## Creating My AWS Account

In this step, I will be setting up my new AWS account. I have signed up and verified my email followed by entering a strong and complex root user password for my account

### Understanding the root user

I learned that a root user is basically a master key to my AWS account and that a strong and complex password is important for the protection of the account as easy to guess passwords or passwords that are weak will be much easier to crack and that way malicious actors are able to gain access to your account. Therefore, the use of strong passwords are important and should be practiced. 

---

## Setting Up Billing and Payment

In this step, I will be setting up my account with details of my contact info as well as payment method for verification purposes. 

### Why AWS requires a payment method

I learned that AWS requires a payment method for the Free Tier as a means of verifying the user and that the verification does not have a charge. 

---

## Completing Account Activation

In this step, I'm have confirmed my identity by verifying my contact details and payment details along with selecting a support plan. For this project, I have selected the basic plan which is free. 

### Identity verification and support plan

I verified my identity by entering my bank card details and proceeded with the basic support plan which includes Customer service for billing and service quota-related questions, personilized view of the health of AWS services and alerts and Access to limited AWS Trusted Advisor checks and guidance.

---

## Enabling Free Tier Alerts

In this step, I have explored the billing preferences tab and enabled free tier usage alerts to allow notifications on the usage of my free tier services. 

![Image](http://learn.nextwork.org/refreshed_navy_happy_tarapirohe/uploads/aws-account-setup_j6k1l7m3)

### Why usage alerts matter

I set up usage alerts because this allows me to be informed on the services that I have in use and to avoid using services which I may not intend to use which will then avoid me from being charged for these services.
